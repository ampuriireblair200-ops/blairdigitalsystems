From Paper to Digital — That's My Mission

Most organizations in Uganda — schools, clinics, SACCOs, supermarkets — are still running on paper, spreadsheets, and manual processes. I come in and change that.

I don't build complex software. I build simple, affordable systems that actually work for people who aren't tech experts. That's my edge.

  • AI-powered chat agents for schools, clinics, and SACCOs
  • Research proposals and academic writing support
  • Digital products and guides sold globally
  • Simple automation systems for small organizations
  • Training organizations to go digital from paper
